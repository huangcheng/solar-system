World cities data bundled in cities.json
==========================================

Source:      SimpleMaps "Basic World Cities Database" (free tier)
             https://simplemaps.com/data/world-cities
Version:     basicv1.75
License:     Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International (CC BY 4.0)
             https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Per the SimpleMaps license, the Basic World Cities Database is free of charge
and licensed under CC BY 4.0, which requires attribution. This bundled copy is
a *derivative* of that database: only cities with population >= 500000 are
retained, exact "City, Country" collisions are deduplicated (keeping the larger
population), and only the fields {name, country, lat, lon} are kept.
